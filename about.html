Hugo Vázquez

Born at Hospital San José on an Easter Sunday of April 11 of 1993, Hugo César Vázquez Martínez is the only son in a family of six. After studying Elementary and Middle School at Instituto Anglia and Highschool at CIDEB, Hugo got his Bachelor's degree in Business Informatics with a certification as a Full Stack Web Developer at Tec de Monterrey and a degree of Bachelor of Music from the Escuela Superior de Música y Danza de Monterrey. He also pursued a Live Sound Production certificate from Audio Experts and a Business Negotiation certificate from the University of Michigan. Hugo is currently working as an IT Consultant and enjoys reading, coding, and videogames.
